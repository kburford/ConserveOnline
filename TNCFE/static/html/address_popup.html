Valid web addresses

What makes a good, and legal, web address (aka "URL")?

In general, aim to make your URL short. For ConserveOnline workspaces, the URL will automatically have http://conserveonline.org/workspaces/ as the starting point for your workspace's URL. The next part of the URL is your workspace name, so keeping it brief is the best.

Next, try to avoid special characters, such as the following:

  • Spaces
  • > and < (greater-than and less-than)
  • # (the pound symbol)
  • " (the quotation mark)
  • % (percent sign)
  • The following: { } | \ ^ ~ [ ] `
  • And these: ; / ? @ = &

These will be "escaped", which means, converted to special codes that are hard to read. It is best to avoid them altogether in your workspace address.
