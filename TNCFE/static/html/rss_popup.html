What is RSS?

RSS stands for "Really Simple Syndication" , and is an XML format that was created to syndicate news, and be a means to share content on the web. Essentially, RSS is a way online for you to get a quick list of the latest story headlines from all your favorite websites and blogs all in one place. RSS feeds contain headlines, summaries, and hyperlinks to complete articles on ConserveOnline and other websites. Users utilize software known as news readers to group headlines from multiple sources into one interface. RSS feeds allow you to easily add headlines from ConserveOnline to your web site. You can also view our RSS feeds in news readers.
