RSS Readers

If you are unable to use a browser to read your RSS feeds or wish to use an external program, there are plenty of good options available. To get started, you need to install a news reader or news aggregator. A wide variety of news aggregators can be found by searching for "news aggregator" in Google or in your favorite search engine. On most browsers, you can right-click the link and select "copy shortcut" or "copy target address" to add our headlines to your news aggregator. Some news aggregators will automatically discover our RSS feeds as you browse. Please consult your news aggregator's documentation for more information.
